Yile Wang is currently an assistant professor at the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University. Previously, He was a postdoc at Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University, working with Prof. Yang Liu. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang University (2018~2022), advised by Prof. Yue Zhang. Before that, he received his B.S./M.S. degree from Zhejiang University and also worked at Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. His primarily research interests include artificial intelligence, natural language processing and large language models.

每年招收一定数量的研究生（含推免生）及本科实习生，欢迎对人工智能、自然语言处理、大语言模型等方向感兴趣，自驱力强的同学与我联系!
